Stance A/B performance

RIQ randomly picks one of three counter-letter stances per analysis (firm-legal, collaborative-evidence, escalation-focused) and records which one was used. Once enough outcomes accumulate, the winning stance per carrier flips on automatically. Low-volume table early on — needs ~50+ outcomes before the per-stance flip rate stabilizes.
