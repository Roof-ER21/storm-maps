## Key insights worth remembering

1. **31–90 days from loss to signed = 82.6% approval rate.** That's the canvasser window to target.
2. **1.25–1.5" hail is a denial valley (52%).** Carriers auto-route to engineer reports (HAAG/Donan) and engineers lean denial. Bring an independent HAAG-certified inspector preemptively in this band.
3. **Adjuster identity > damage.** Per industry research, same property + different IA = opposite outcomes. We have 364 named adjusters tracked.
4. **PA matching law is adverse** (Greene v. USAA 2007). MD has the strongest bad-faith statute (§27-303). VA falls in between.
5. **15-year roof age is a hard cliff,** not gradual. Carrier reinspection trigger drives decisions.
